About Me

I completed my M.S. in Astronomy & Astrophysics at IIST, and now in a transition phase. I’m passionate about exploring the physics of galaxies and black holes, integrating data science and machine learning into astrophysical research. I’ve worked extensively with multi-wavelength datasets, including UVIT/AstroSat and MIRI MRS/JWST, and have experience with tools like CCDLAB, CubeViz, and 3D-Barolo.

Outside academia, I enjoy cubing, writing, and traveling.

Research & Projects

  • AGN Feedback in Nearby Galaxies: Studying molecular gas dynamics using JWST and ALMA data.
  • Machine Learning for X-ray Binary Classification: Applying ensemble models for classifying HMXBs, LMXBs, CVs, and pulsars.
  • Star Formation History Mapping: Constructing internal extinction-corrected UV color maps.
